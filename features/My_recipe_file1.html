"AUTHENTIC SOUTH INDIAN EGG CURRY"

"This is my kids' favorite curry"
This is an Authentic South Indian recipe from my Grandma's Kitchen

Preparation Time:

This recipe takes 45 min to prepare.
Prep time : 10 min.
Cooking time : 35 min.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp -cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp - black pepper
  • 1 tsp - poppy seeds
  • 1 tsp - coriander
  • 1 tsp - turmeric
  • 2 - cloves
  • 1 - cinnamon
  • 2 - green chillies
  • 5 - 10 dry red chilies (as per the preferred level of spice)
  • 2tsp -Ginger and Garlic past
  • 1 - tomato
  • 1 - onion
  • 2 - strings of curry leaves
  • 1 tsp - fennel seeds
  • 1/4 cup -coriander leaves
  • 5 - 7 boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup - shredded coconut
  • 2 tsp - coconut oil
  • 2 tsp - Salt
  • 2 cup - Water as per needed
  • 1/2 tsp - Garam masala powder

Preparation Method:

Heat up a pot or a vessel Start dry roasting cinnamon, cloves, cumin seed, poppy seeds, black pepper, coriander seeds, and fennel seeds. Let the ingredients dry roast for 2 minutes. Add curry leaves and green chillies and red chillies and roast it for two more minutes Add turmeric powder (put in a bowl or plate separately until it cools down). Add a spoon of coconut oil in the same pot Fry the onion until it turns golden brown. Add ginger and garlic and fry it a little. Add the tomatoes and fry it a bit. Switch of the pot and add coconuts Stir everything together. Put everything into a mixer. Add the mixture that we left to cool into the pot.

Grind all of the ingredients together (Finely). Boil eggs and peel them, Keep them separate for later. Take the same vessel and pour one more spoon of coconut oil and add a little bit of curry leaves. Add the paste that we grinder into the pot, along with one cup of water Stir everything together. Put salt as per the need, Stir everything together and let it boil for five minutes.Cut the eggs in half and add them to the mixture. Add a little more water and a garam masala powder. Close it and let it boil for 5-10 minutes.

When you see the oil separating from the gravy, stop the stove and put the chopped coriander leaves into the gravy, Serve it hot with rice!, chapatti! or naan!. Whatever you prefer. Enjoy!
